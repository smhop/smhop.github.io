What is a gacha game?
"A gacha game (Japanese: ガチャ ゲーム, Hepburn: gacha gēmu) is a video game that implements the gachapon machine style mechanics. Similar to loot boxes,"
Gacha game, Wikipedia
Some gacha games I like are the Hoyoverse games,
  • Honkai Impact 3rd
  • Genshin Impact
  • Honkai: Star Rail
  • Zenless Zone Zero
I like these because of the games' story and gameplay. All of these games, except for ZZZ (to an extent) are a part of a multiversal plot, where each game's story influences the others. My favorite is HI3 because of the main character, Kiana Kaslana. Her story was genuinely one of the best I have ever seen in media. She changed the lives of everyone around her for the goal of protecting all things beautiful in the world and fighting against anything that threatens it.

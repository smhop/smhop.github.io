I was born in Manila, Philippines. My mother is Filipino and my Dad is American, so I had both a Filipino and American Passport since I was young. My Dad would visit over Christmas and I would visit him over summer break.

I started school at 3 when I went to a sort of daycare program and started learning to read and write. I stayed in that program up to the pre-Kindergarten level when I was 4 years old and started going to a Catholic School for Kindergarten-3rd grade when I turned 5. When I was 7-8 years old, I moved to America and started 4th grade here in Hawaii.

I'm glad I took this class because I've always liked to code. I was in a coding club in elementary school, took a computer class in middle school, and now another computer class in high school. If I had known about this class sooner I would've taken it since Freshman year.

After high School, I plan to go to community college for an associates degree in computer science before getting a job and working before transferring my credits to UH for a bachelor's degree.
